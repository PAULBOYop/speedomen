Welcome to speeDomen

At speeDomen, we dedicate our hearts and souls to everything we create. We live by our core values every day to keep delivering epic entertainment experiences to all our players. It doesn't matter who you are or where you're located—whether you're part of our dynamic and growing community, working with us at one of our offices worldwide, or carrying a piece of speeDomen with you into space—you belong here.

What We Stand For

Dedicated to delivering the most epic entertainment experiences... ever. speeDomen's Entertainment eight core values represent the principles and beliefs that have guided our company throughout the years. These values are reflected in employees' decisions and actions every day.

Gameplay First

Everything we do at speeDomen revolves around the success of the gaming experiences we offer our players. The aim of each department within the company—whether it’s art, programming, or customer support—is to make our games as enjoyable as possible for the widest audience we can reach.

Commit to quality

"speeDomen polish" applies not just to our gameplay experiences but to every aspect of our work. We approach each task with care and seriousness. We seek honest feedback and use it to enhance the quality of our work. Ultimately, most players won't remember if the game was late—only if it was great.

Play Nice; Play Fair

First impressions matter in our business, but lasting impressions are everything. We aim to uphold a high level of respect and integrity in all our interactions with players, colleagues, and business partners. The behavior of every speeDomen employee, both online and offline, can impact the entire company.

Every Voice Matters

"Great ideas can come from anywhere. Blizzard Entertainment is what it is today because of the voices of our players and of each member of the company. Every employee is encouraged to speak up, listen, be respectful of other opinions, and embrace criticism as just another avenue for great ideas.

Learn & Grow

The games industry is ever-changing. Technology improves, techniques change, and design philosophies become outdated. Since the founding of Blizzard Entertainment, we’ve worked to improve through experience, teaching one another and cultivating the desire to be the best at what we do. We see this as an individual responsibility as well as a company one. Employees can count on their peers, managers, and the company itself to be supportive and help them gain the knowledge and training they need.

Embrace Your inner Greeks

Everyone here is a geek at heart. Cutting-edge technology, comic books, science fiction, high-end video cards, action figures with the kung-fu grip... Whatever they're passionate about, it matters that each employee embraces it! Their unique enthusiasm helps shape the fun, creative culture that is speeDomen.

Join Us at speeCon

speeCon is where we celebrate the best community of teammates and guildmates in the known universe. Featuring esports, games, cosplay, music, community meetups, and much more, speeCon is a place where geeks, players, and employees from all walks of life can come together and feel at home.
